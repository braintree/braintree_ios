Objective-C

@interface BTTokenizationService : NSObject

Swift

class BTTokenizationService : NSObject

A tokenization service that supports registration of tokenizers at runtime.

BTTokenizationService provides access to tokenization services from payment options (e.g. BTPayPalDriver) without introducing compile-time dependencies on the frameworks.
